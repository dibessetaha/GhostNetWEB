GhostNets Application

Welcome to GhostNets

GhostNets is a web application dedicated to tracking and salvaging abandoned fishing nets, commonly known as "ghost nets." Our platform provides a centralized system for reporting, managing, and retrieving ghost nets from the sea. Together, we can make a positive impact on marine life and the environment.

Key Features

  • Efficient Reporting: Report ghost nets anonymously or as a salvaging individual.
  • Salvaging Coordination: Coordinate and redistribute salvages to avoid unnecessary trips.
  • Real-time Tracking: View the status of ghost nets on a world map.
  • Missing Nets: Report ghost nets as missing to streamline search efforts.

Benefits

  • Environmental Impact: Contribute to the preservation of marine ecosystems by removing ghost nets.
  • Community Collaboration: Connect with like-minded individuals to collectively address the issue of ghost nets.
  • Efficiency and Transparency: Streamline the reporting and salvaging process with a user-friendly and transparent platform.
